Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2026

Tornix ("we", "our", or "us") is operated by Zindigon Enterprises. This policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use the Tornix app.

Information We Collect

  • Name and email address for account creation
  • Payment information processed securely through Stripe
  • Tournament activity including registrations, results, and bracket data
  • Device information for push notifications

How We Use Your Information

  • To create and manage your account
  • To process tournament entry fees and prize payouts via Stripe
  • To send notifications about your tournaments and matches
  • To display tournament brackets and results

Third Party Services

  • Firebase — used for authentication and push notifications
  • Stripe — used for payment processing. Your payment data is handled directly by Stripe and never stored on our servers.
  • Supabase — used for database and backend services

Data Retention

We retain your data for as long as your account is active. You may request deletion of your account and associated data by contacting us.

Contact

For privacy questions or data deletion requests, contact us at: privacy@zindigon.com
